🔮 Frequently Asked Questions 🔮

❓ Is this reading accurate?

Many find these readings insightful, but interpretation is key. 🪄

🔍 How do I start my reading?

Click "Begin" to start. The next card to select will glow, guiding you through the process. ✨

📜 What type of reading is this?

This is a unique variation of the Celtic Cross, passed down through generations. It offers insights into your past, present, and potential futures. Important: Don't ask a specific question. The cards will reveal what's most relevant.

🤷‍♂️ Why was this created?

This is an evolution of a long-standing reading service, offered both in-person and online. It has been refined over many years and various platforms. 💻

🃏 How does this compare to physical readings?

Users report similar accuracy between these digital readings and traditional physical card readings. 🎴

⚙️ Tips for Interpretation

  • 👑 Avoid gender assumptions based on card titles.
  • 🔄 Read in a spiral from the center, connecting each card to previous ones.
  • 🧩 Consider card positions for deeper understanding.
  • 🔮 The "root cause" card may seem unrelated but often proves significant.
  • 💡 The "possible outcome" card is more for insight than prediction.
  • ❤️ Keep the "Heart of the Matter" card in mind throughout the reading.

🔄 How often should I get a reading?

We recommend no more than one reading per day. Multiple readings in quick succession may lose coherence and accuracy. 🕒

💸 Why is this service free?

This project is about sharing insights, not generating profit. It's offered freely in the spirit of 1337 H4x. 💖

🃏 What does changing the deck do?

Modifies the graphics being used for the front and back of cards. 🃏

🛠️ Technical Details

Built with HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript. It's open-source and freely distributable. Contribute on GitHub. There is even a handy tool to scrape and add new deck graphics. 🌐
